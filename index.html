About Us

Keep your fleet airborne with the help of Aero-Craft Hydraulics, Inc of Corona, California. We repair and sell aircraft components and parts worldwide. We take pride in our ability to work on a large variety of hydraulic applications. Our experienced staff evaluates, repairs, maintains, and overhauls a wide variety of components to meet your needs, including everything from main engine pumps to actuators.

We are a minority-owned FAA repair station focused on providing you with quality service at affordable rates. For your convenience, we also sell a wide variety of essential components from some of the world's most trusted manufacturers. We serve the airline component industry in ATA Chapters 25, 27, 28, 29, 32, 36, 52, and 73. Contact us right away to learn how we can assist you.
